Common Capabilities

Every vertical app is AI-ready on day one

All 9 applications ship with IQVigilant pre-configured — no integration work, no API keys to wire, no model training. The AI console is running the moment you veloiq run.

💬

1-User Natural Language Queries

One decision-maker per app (CCO, CISO, Medical Director, VP of Sales, Plant Manager…) queries their data in plain English. Pre-configured NL chats ship with every vertical — no prompt engineering required.

🚨

10 Exception Alerts

Automated alerting for critical conditions in your domain — transaction risk thresholds, SOC2 report expiries, SLA breaches, OEE drops, ROAS underperformance, and more. IQVigilant detects and notifies.

🎯

100 Personalization Rules

Tailor the application to your organization’s workflows with up to 100 custom business rules — field constraints, derived formulas, conditional visibility, and automated actions. Declared in JSON, enforced at runtime.

🔒

Local-First Privacy

All data, schemas, and credentials stay on your infrastructure. IQVigilant runs locally — your clinical, financial, and security data never leaves your environment.

🤖

Bring Your Own LLM

Use OpenAI, Anthropic, WatsonX, or local models via Ollama. IQVigilant’s Native Answering Method runs deterministic queries with zero hallucinations when LLMs aren’t needed.

📊

Instant Visual Results

Charts, graphs, metric widgets, and dashboards rendered automatically — bar, line, pie, donut, scatter, bubble, heatmap, waterfall, and more. Every NL query produces a visual answer.
